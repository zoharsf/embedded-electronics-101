    📱 Useful Mobile Apps¶

    For Arduino/ESP32¶

    • Arduino IoT Cloud Remote (iOS/Android) - Control your ESP32 remotely
    • Serial Bluetooth Terminal (Android) - Bluetooth communication
    • Blynk (iOS/Android) - Build custom IoT interfaces

    Tools¶

    • ElectroDroid (Android) - Component lookup, calculators
    • Circuit Calculator (iOS) - Resistor values, Ohm's law
    • Resistor Scanner (iOS/Android) - Identify resistors by photo

    🔬 Simulators (Practice Without Hardware)¶

    • Wokwi (wokwi.com) - ESP32 simulator (runs real code!)
    • Tinkercad Circuits (tinkercad.com) - Arduino simulator
    • Falstad Circuit Simulator (falstad.com) - Electronic circuit simulator

    📚 Recommended Books¶

    Beginners¶

    • "Make: Electronics" by Charles Platt
    • "Arduino Project Handbook" by Mark Geddes
    • "Getting Started with Arduino" by Massimo Banzi

    Intermediate¶

    • "Programming Arduino" by Simon Monk
    • "ESP32 Projects" by Agus Kurniawan
    • "Practical Electronics for Inventors" by Paul Scherz

    Advanced¶

    • "The Art of Electronics" by Horowitz & Hill (the bible!)
    • "Designing Embedded Hardware" by John Catsoulis
