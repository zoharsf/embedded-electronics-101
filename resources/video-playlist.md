## Recommended for Pre-Workshop

## Pro Tips for Video Learning

1. **Pause and rewind** - Don't worry about getting it all in one watch
2. **Watch multiple videos on same topic** - Different explanations click differently
3. **Speed up if you're comfortable** - YouTube's 1.25x/1.5x speed is great
4. **Take notes** - Write down key concepts
5. **Rewatch before workshops** - Your brain needs reinforcement!
